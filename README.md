# Dynamic Pricing Engine
# Overview
The Dynamic Pricing Engine is an AI-powered pricing optimization platform that automatically adjusts product prices in real-time based on multiple factors including demand, competition, inventory levels, customer behavior, and market conditions. This system helps businesses maximize revenue, improve competitiveness, and respond quickly to market changes.
Key Features

Real-time Price Optimization: Automatically adjust prices based on current market conditions and business rules
Competitor Price Monitoring: Track competitor pricing across multiple channels and adjust accordingly
Demand-based Pricing: AI algorithms analyze demand patterns and customer behavior to optimize pricing
Inventory-aware Pricing: Adjust prices based on stock levels to prevent overstock or stockouts
Price Elasticity Analysis: Understand how price changes impact demand for different products
Rule-based Constraints: Set minimum/maximum prices, margin requirements, and business rules
A/B Testing: Test different pricing strategies and measure their impact on revenue
Multi-channel Support: Synchronize pricing across e-commerce, retail, and wholesale channels
Analytics Dashboard: Visualize pricing performance, revenue impact, and market trends

# Technology Stack

Machine Learning: scikit-learn, LightGBM, Python Database Connector,
Time Series, Feature Engineering, Matplotlib, Seaborn.
Database: TSQL with time-series optimization in Microsoft SQL server
Visual Platform: Tableau 

# Pricing Strategies Supported

Competitive Pricing: Match or beat competitor prices
Penetration Pricing: Lower prices to gain market share
Premium Pricing: Higher prices for perceived value
Time-based Pricing: Adjust prices based on time of day, week, or season
Surge Pricing: Increase prices during high-demand periods
Clearance Pricing: Aggressive pricing to move excess inventory
Psychological Pricing: Optimize price points for customer perception
